# Request for Proposal Bode for Peekaboo (RFP)  
**Wireless Infrastructure Around the Office Network**

---

## Information about the Organization  
Peekaboo is a growing retail and logistics company with a strong focus on digital transformation. We are seeking proposals from qualified vendors to design, deploy, and maintain a robust wireless infrastructure across our office premises to support high-performance connectivity, security, and scalability.

---

## Project Scope

### Objectives  
- Deliver seamless wireless coverage across all office areas  
- Support high-density usage with modern wireless standards  
- Integrate with our existing wired infrastructure  
- Ensure secure, scalable, and manageable wireless access  
- Provide long-term support and performance monitoring

---

### Scope of Work  
1. **Site Survey & Planning**  
   - Conduct a physical and RF site survey  
   - Identify optimal access point (AP) placement  
   - Provide a heatmap and coverage plan

2. **Installation & Configuration**  
   - Install enterprise-grade wireless access points (minimum Wi-Fi 6)  
   - Configure SSIDs, VLANs, and QoS policies  
   - Enable WPA3 encryption and MAC address filtering

3. **Network Integration**  
   - Seamless integration with existing wired LAN  
   - Configure DHCP, DNS, and routing as needed  
   - Ensure VLAN tagging and switch compatibility

4. **Security Implementation**  
   - Implement RADIUS or 802.1X authentication  
   - Enable rogue AP detection and isolation  
   - Apply firewall rules and intrusion detection

5. **Monitoring & Management**  
   - Deploy centralized wireless controller or cloud dashboard  
   - Enable real-time monitoring, alerting, and analytics  
   - Provide monthly performance and usage reports

6. **Maintenance & Support**  
   - Provide 12-month support with SLAs  
   - Include firmware updates and hardware replacement  
   - Offer optional extended support contracts

---

## Technical Requirements

### Coverage  
- 100% coverage across all office zones, including meeting rooms, break areas, and storage  
- Signal strength ≥ -65 dBm in all user-accessible areas

### Performance  
- Support for Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) or higher  
- Minimum 1 Gbps backhaul per AP  
- Capacity to handle 100+ simultaneous devices per AP

### Security  
- WPA3 encryption  
- MAC address filtering  
- Guest network isolation  
- Scheduled vulnerability scans

### Management  
- Web-based or cloud-based controller  
- Role-based access control for IT staff  
- SNMP or API integration for NOC tools

---

## Deliverables  
- Site survey report and heatmap  
- Network design and implementation plan  
- Configured and deployed wireless infrastructure  
- Documentation of configurations and topology
